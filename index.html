Enhancing Robotic Precision and Adaptability

The primary focus of the project is on improving the robot's mechanical and software systems. Through kinematic optimization, we enhance the robot's precision, mobility, and adaptability. We analyze and test various parameters, implementing innovative solutions that improve the robot's operational performance.

The purpose of the project

Optimizing robot kinematics to ensure precise movements and maneuverability.

Enhancing the control mechanism with software and technical updates for optimal robot management.

Our work will be based on kinematic calculations and testing processes aimed at optimizing the robot on both technical and software levels.

Our Plan

1

Kinematic analysis: Analyzing motion systems for flaws.

2

Mechanical improvements: Modifying components to enhance motion.

3

Testing: Real-world trials.
